Admission Timeline 2026

Important dates and milestones for the admission process

January 15 - February 28

Application Period

Online applications open for all UG and PG programs. Submit your application with required documents and entrance exam details.

March 10-15

Entrance Examination

College-specific entrance test for merit-based admissions. JEE/CET scores also considered for eligible candidates.

March 25

Result Declaration

Merit list published based on entrance exam scores and academic performance. Shortlisted candidates notified for counseling.

April 5-15

Counseling & Seat Allocation

Document verification and seat allocation process. Candidates can choose preferred branches based on merit and availability.

April 20-30

Fee Payment & Admission Confirmation

Payment of admission fees and submission of original documents for final confirmation of admission.

Eligibility Criteria

Check if you meet the requirements for your desired program

🎓

B.Tech Programs

  • 10+2 with PCM/PCB from recognized board
  • Minimum 45% marks (40% for reserved categories)
  • Valid JEE Main/CET/COMEDK score
  • Age limit: 17-25 years as on admission date
  • Domicile: Karnataka/All India quota available
🎯

M.Tech Programs

  • B.E/B.Tech in relevant discipline
  • Minimum 50% marks (45% for reserved)
  • Valid GATE score preferred
  • PGCET score accepted
  • No age limit for postgraduate programs
📚

Ph.D Programs

  • Master's degree in relevant field
  • Minimum 55% marks (50% for reserved)
  • Valid NET/GATE/UGC score
  • Research proposal required
  • Interview and presentation mandatory
🌍

International Students

  • Valid student visa required
  • English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL)
  • Equivalence certificate from AIU
  • NOC from concerned authorities
  • Separate international quota available
