Admission Process

  1. Registration: Create an account on the college/counselling portal using a valid email/mobile number.
  2. Application Form: Fill in personal, academic (10th/12th marks), and entrance exam details; some info auto-fills.
  3. Document Upload: Scan and upload required documents (Photo, 10th/12th marksheet, entrance exam rank card, category/income certificates) in specified formats (JPG, PDF, etc.).
  4. Entrance Exam & Merit: Admissions rely heavily on qualifying exams like JEE Main/Advanced, Karnataka CET, COMEDK, or university-specific tests (e.g., KNET).
  5. Online Counselling: Participate in centralized counselling (JoSAA for IITs, KEA for Karnataka) for seat allocation based on rank.
  6. Fee Payment: Pay application fees and then provisional admission/tuition fees online via net banking/cards after seat allotment.
  7. Document Verification: Submit original documents at the allotted institute for final confirmation.
